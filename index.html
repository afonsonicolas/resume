About Nicolas


Born in Miami and raised in Miami, Colombia, Brazil, and Switzerland, Nicolas comes from a multicultural background with family roots from Spain and Costa Rica.  He speaks fluent English, Spanish, Portuguese, and is midway towards German proficiency. Beyond his curiosity for diversity of culture and thought, Nicolas' overseas upbringing sparked an interest in International Relations and Public Relations; to understand how messages and communication impact people of different experiences.

Nicolas recently completed a bachelor's degree in Public Relations (BS) and International Relations (BA) with a minor in Business Administration at Boston University (May '23) and wants to apply his expertise to further the purpose of a strategy, communications, and marketing team.

Most recently, Nicolas served as a consultant for crisis management at Grasso Group Communications in Boston, MA. Concurrently, he worked as a pro-bono PR manager for Service Dog Project, a charity organization that breeds, trains, and donates service animals to communities in need. Nicolas has also worked in B2B PR and business development for engineering firm, Aspen Systems (Boston, MA), where he expanded the organization's shared and owned media channels through value-driven content as well as providing strategic recommendations to diversify revenue streams.

Certified in SEO, analytics, and inbound marketing, Nicolas has previously worked in luxury PR for Müller JG Jewelry in San Jose, Costa Rica as website and marketing strategy developer. Prior, he worked in health PR as an in-house communications intern for Sandoz (São Paulo, Brazil), a global leader in generic and bio-similar Pharma.

At Boston University, Nicolas has achieved the Dean's List award for six consecutive semesters. He has sat on the board of International Business Club, and has served as a chair for Model United Nations (MUN). During his time at MUN, Nicolas has received six public-speaking awards in a span of ten conferences attended.

After work, Nicolas enjoys reading the news and catching up on current politics, finance, and relevant industry trends. Despite his lack of a green thumb, Nicolas satisfies his love for plants and Legos by building Lego® Botanical sets and displaying them around the house. In his free time, Nicolas is an avid skier during the winter season and enjoys playing golf during warmer months.
